PM Internship Scheme

Government of India Initiative

Connecting Indian Youth with Quality Internship Opportunities

A government initiative designed to bridge the gap between talented students and industry opportunities across India. We support first-generation learners, rural youth, and students from remote areas in finding relevant internships that match their skills and aspirations.
